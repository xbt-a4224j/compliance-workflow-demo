Performance Highlights — Q1 2026

ABOUT THE FUND

The Summit Blue-Chip Fund invests in established large-capitalization U.S.
companies known for stable earnings, durable market positioning, and
consistent dividend history.

PERFORMANCE

The Fund has delivered strong returns since its 2019 inception, with a
trailing five-year annualized return of 12.4%. Past performance is not a
guarantee of future results.

ABOUT RISK

Investing in equities involves risk. Market conditions, sector rotation, and
interest rate movements can affect the value of your investment over the
short and intermediate term. The Fund seeks to mitigate volatility through
disciplined position sizing and quarterly rebalancing across more than fifty
holdings.

FEES AND EXPENSES

The Fund's annual management fee is 0.45%, with a total annual expense
ratio of 0.55%, calculated daily and disclosed in the Fee Schedule on
page 3 of the prospectus.
