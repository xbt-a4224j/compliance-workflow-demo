NORTHWIND CAPITAL GROWTH FUND
Fact Sheet — Q1 2026

STRATEGY

The Northwind Capital Growth Fund seeks long-term capital appreciation by
investing in a diversified portfolio of large-cap U.S. equities. Our
proprietary screening process targets companies with sustainable competitive
advantages and proven management teams.

PERFORMANCE

Over the past three years, the Fund has delivered a 14.2% annualized return.
The five-year return stands at 11.8%, and the trailing twelve months produced
gains of 18.4%. Investors who held since inception have seen total returns
of more than 60%.

RISKS

Investing in the Fund involves substantial risks, including the possibility
that you may lose some or all of your principal investment. The value of
equity securities can fluctuate significantly in response to market
conditions, company-specific events, and broader economic trends.

FEES AND EXPENSES

The Fund charges a 0.65% annual management fee and 0.05% other expenses,
for a total annual expense ratio of 0.70%.
